========================================
COMPANY POLICIES AND GUIDELINES
========================================

ABOUT COMPANY:
We are a fast-growing technology company focused on building innovative 
software solutions. Our mission is to empower businesses through technology.

CORE VALUES:
- Innovation: We embrace new ideas and technologies
- Integrity: We do the right thing, always
- Collaboration: We work together to achieve success
- Excellence: We strive for the highest quality
- Respect: We value each other's perspectives

WORK POLICIES:

1. WORK HOURS:
   - Standard hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (IST)
   - Flexible hours available (core hours 11 AM to 4 PM)
   - 2 days work from home per week (post probation)
   - Full remote work available for senior positions

2. ATTENDANCE:
   - 12 casual leaves per year
   - 18 privilege leaves per year
   - 10 sick leaves per year
   - Leave policy must be followed with proper intimation

3. CODE OF CONDUCT:
   - Maintain professional behavior at all times
   - Respect colleagues and their opinions
   - No discrimination based on gender, race, religion, or age
   - Zero tolerance for harassment or bullying
   - Maintain confidentiality of company and client information

4. COMMUNICATION:
   - Use professional language in all communications
   - Slack/Teams for day-to-day communication
   - Email for formal communications
   - Response expected within 24 hours during working days

5. DRESS CODE:
   - Smart casual for office
   - Professional attire for client meetings
   - Video calls: Formals preferred

6. SECURITY:
   - Never share passwords
   - Use company VPN when accessing sensitive data
   - Lock your system when away
   - Report any security concerns immediately

7. PERFORMANCE:
   - Quarterly performance reviews
   - Annual appraisal cycle
   - Clear KPIs and OKRs
   - Regular feedback sessions with manager

8. LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT:
   - Annual learning budget of Rs. 50,000 per employee
   - Support for certifications
   - Internal tech talks and knowledge sharing sessions
   - Access to online learning platforms

9. DEVICE AND EQUIPMENT:
   - Company laptop provided to all employees
   - Monthly internet reimbursement for WFH
   - Equipment must be returned upon resignation

10. SOCIAL MEDIA:
    - Do not share confidential company information
    - Be respectful when posting about company
    - Personal opinions should be clearly marked as personal

11. REMOTE WORK GUIDELINES:
    - Ensure stable internet connection
    - Attend all mandatory meetings via video
    - Maintain regular communication with team
    - Create a dedicated workspace
    - Be available during core working hours

12. MEETINGS:
    - Be on time for all meetings
    - Camera on during video meetings
    - Share agenda before meetings
    - Take notes and follow up on action items

13. PROBATION:
    - Duration: 6 months
    - Performance review at 3 months
    - Confirmation based on performance
    - Notice period during probation: 1 month

14. RESIGNATION:
    - Notice period: 2 months (after confirmation)
    - Exit interview mandatory
    - Clearance process must be completed
    - Full and final settlement within 30 days

15. CONFLICT RESOLUTION:
    - Address issues directly with the person involved
    - If unresolved, escalate to manager
    - HR available for mediation
    - Follow the escalation hierarchy

========================================
Thank you for being part of our team!
